Get Your Permit

An easy way to get street occupancy permits in the City of Boston. Built for the HubHacks Hackathon in Boston on August 2014.

Built with:

  • Bootstrap front-end design framework.
  • JavaScript - Jquery and Node.js
  • Bootswatch themes.
  • CouchDB database
  • Twilio SMS
